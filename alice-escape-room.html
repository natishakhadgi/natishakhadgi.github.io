ALICE'S CASTLE ESCAPE

Team: Stephanie Vang [Artist], Julie Wei [Artist]
Role: Programmer
Tools: Unity, C#
Timeline: 4 Weeks [Nov 2020 - Dec 2020]

Alice’s Castle Escape is a virtial escape room inspired by Lewis Carroll’s novel, Alice in Wonderland. During the Covid-19 pandemic, I spent a lot of time playing virtual escape rooms as an escape from reality. I decided to create a simple, fun escape room with two other artists.
