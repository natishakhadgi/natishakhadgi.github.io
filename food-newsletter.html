FOOD NEWSLETTER

Team: Teddy Ha [Creative Director], Timbrah Northrip [Layout], David Koster [Photographer], Writers
Role: Editor
Tools: Adobe InDesign
Timeline: 4 Weeks [Nov 2018 - Dec 2018]

Food Newsletter newsletter created by and for San Jose State University's International House's Fall 2018 residents. This newsletter contains nine recipes found all over the globe. Each semester, a creative director is in charge of creating a newsletter based on a selected theme. I worked closely with the director of the International House and the creative director to create a fun newsletter that features rich history from our residents alongside delicious recipes.
